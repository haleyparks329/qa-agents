# Saved Cart Reminder

Simulated feature request for a demo ecommerce app. Returning shoppers should see a reminder when they have items left in their cart from an earlier session.

- Show a non-blocking banner when a mock shopper signs in with saved cart items.
- Let the shopper continue checkout or dismiss the reminder.
- Do not show the reminder after the cart has been emptied.
- Keep cart totals unchanged when the reminder is displayed.
